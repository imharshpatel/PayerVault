Welcome to the Free Fire Tournament!

Join our epic Free Fire tournaments and showcase your skills against top players.

Tournament Rules

  1. Player Must be level 50 or higher
  2. Participants must fill correct details while registering
  3. All players must join the match using the provided room ID and password.
  4. Cheating or unauthorized behaviour will result in disqualification and a permanent ban from future tournaments.
  5. Team up will lead to disqualification and ban from future tournament, rest of the players will get chance to play next round for free
  6. Players must respect each other and maintain good sportsmanship.
  7. Decisions made by the tournament organizers are final.

Notice

  1. Entry charges of all players will be refunded if any hacker is found in the game
  2. Visit the Support page for any concerns related to the tournament.
