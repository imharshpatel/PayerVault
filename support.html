Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register for a tournament?

You can register by clicking the "Register Now" button on the tournament details page.

2. What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

Please contact our support team via email at infoplayervault@gmail.com

3. How are the winners determined?

Winners are determined based on in-game performance as per the tournament rules.

4. What if a hacker is found in the game?

If a hacker is found in the game, your entry fee will be refunded, or you will get a chance to play the next round of tournaments for free.

5. What are the entry fees for tournaments?

Entry fees vary by tournament. Please check the tournament details for specific fees.

6. How will I receive my tournament schedule?

Your tournament schedule will be emailed to you after registration is complete.
